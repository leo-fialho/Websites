About Us

C.T. Moffitt & Company, a management consulting and interim management firm, was founded in 1985. Headquartered in Beverly Hills, California, C.T. Moffitt & Company has undertaken assignments on the U.S. West Coast and around the globe. The common theme of the firm's engagements has been to assist companies in transition ranging from startups to well established enterprises in need of fresh perspective, to companies on the brink of bankruptcy. Borrowing on the broad experience of its principals and allied consultants, the firm provides services in the areas of corporate performance improvement, turnaround management, financial restructuring, and interim management. Some of the industries served by C.T. Moffitt & Company have included internet commerce, hand tools, specialty retail, steel manufacturing & fabrication, computer hardware and software, consumer electronics, telecommunications, furniture manufacturing, motion picture post production, food importing and distribution, real estate, medical technology and apparel.


Charles T. "Chuck" Moffitt

Over more than two decades, Chuck Moffitt has been a CEO, COO or CFO in sixteen companies in diverse industries, combining skills in marketing, finance, strategic planning, and personnel management. He has also served on nine boards of directors, arranged numerous mergers and equity financings, and structured senior debt packages of up to $100 million. Mr. Moffitt is a member of the Turnaround Management Association, the Association of Insolvency and Restructuring Advisors, the American Bankruptcy Institute, the Commercial Finance Association, and the Association for Corporate Growth. Prior to founding C.T. Moffitt & Company, Chuck served as Executive Assistant to the Mayor (Deputy Mayor) of the City of Los Angeles directly responsible for policy guidance of twenty city departments with 18,000 personnel and budgets in excess of $4 billion. Earlier in his career, Chuck was a Vice President at First Interstate Bank where he managed major corporate relationships including Times Mirror, Southern California Edison and Mattel. From 1967-1975, Chuck held positions in the Federal Government as Special Assistant to the Majority Leadership of the U.S. House of Representatives and earlier as a Foreign Service Officer with the Department of State, serving as U.S. Consul in Recife, Brazil, and as Chief of Staff to the U.S. Ambassador to Brazil. Chuck is a graduate of UCLA. He earned his M.A. at the University of Washington and pursued doctoral studies at the University of California, Berkeley.
